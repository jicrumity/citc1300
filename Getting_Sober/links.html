Resources for Finding Rehab

Explore Rehabilitation Options

If you or a loved one is seeking help for addiction, finding the right rehabilitation center is crucial. Here are some reliable resources to help you locate treatment facilities and support services:

    Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA)
  • Find Treatment
  • This government resource allows you to search for local treatment options based on your location and needs.
      • National Institute on Drug Abuse (NIDA)
  • NIDA
  • NIDA provides information on different treatment approaches and links to additional resources for finding help.
      • Addiction Center
  • The Addiction Center
  • This site offers a comprehensive directory of rehab facilities, along with articles and guides about addiction and recovery.
      • Rehabs.com
  • Rehabs

      • American Addiction Centers
  • Centers
  • This organization provides a directory of treatment facilities and resources for those seeking recovery.
